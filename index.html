About

Seven-plus years at the intersection of strategy and delivery.

I've progressed from hands-on programme delivery into senior leadership — designing, funding and scaling initiatives across youth employment, girls' education, women's livelihoods and civic engagement. Focus Areas

Where I concentrate my work

01

Youth Development

Opportunity access for a dignified life — connecting employment, income, learning and skills-building at the intersection of education and work, so young people move toward dignified opportunities.

02

Education Equity

Access, gender-responsive budgeting and continuity of learning in fragile and conflict-affected communities.

03

Women's Economic Empowerment

Livelihood, agribusiness and financial-literacy programming that moves women toward dignified income.

04

Programme Design & Grant Management

Strategy, proposal development and donor partnership across multi-project portfolios.

05

Policy Advocacy & Governance

06

Youth Leadership Ecosystems

Building YouthXcel Africa — an ecosystem connecting young people to purpose, skills and income together.

Global Youth Advocacy
