Impact Portfolio

Results I can point to, not just roles I've held.

A record of what programmes I've led actually changed — for the people in them and the systems around them.

$90,000 Embassy funding

WAVES — Women Against Violence, Empowered through Sustainability

Designed and oversaw this initiative, securing over $90,000 from the Canadian and French embassies to move 600+ young women in high-risk communities through agribusiness, financial literacy and climate-smart livelihood training.

Adopted by state government

Gender-Responsive Education Budgeting Framework, Kano State

Directed the research and advocacy behind Kano State's first Gender-Responsive Education Budgeting Framework — formally adopted by the state government to guide allocation of education resources toward girls and vulnerable learners.

Passed into law

Kano State Child Protection Law

Facilitated cross-sector dialogue among civil society, policymakers and international partners that contributed to the passage of Kano State's Child Protection Law.

39,000+ young people

Young Africa Works — Mastercard Foundation

Localised a youth employability strategy under the Young Africa Works initiative, strengthening job-readiness for over 39,000 young people across Kano State.

4,000+ proposals reviewed

EU Youth Empowerment Fund — Global Review Panel

As Senior Youth Panelist, evaluated over 4,000 youth-led project applications globally against scalability, sustainability and community-relevance criteria, and delivered structured feedback on 75+ shortlisted proposals shaping €10 million in resource allocation.

3.7M jobs targeted

Nigeria Youth Employment Action Plan Framework

Contributed to a national policy effort targeting 3.7 million jobs for young Nigerians, and co-authored five policy briefs on women's empowerment, youth leadership and education.

1,500+ youth connected

YouthXcel Africa Ecosystem

Founded and lead a youth empowerment ecosystem connecting young people to purpose discovery, practical skill-building and income opportunities across underserved communities in Nigeria.
